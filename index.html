How to Play

Simple to learn, satisfying to master.

1

Draw Your Cards

A fresh hand of letter cards is dealt each round. No two games play out the same way.

2

Spell a Word

Arrange cards from your hand to form valid English words and play them onto the board.

3

Clear the Board

Use all your cards wisely to clear the board and progress to the next level.

🧠 Grows Your Vocabulary

Every puzzle pushes you to recall and form real words under pressure — a natural, enjoyable way to expand your vocabulary over time.

🎯 Daily Rewards & Streaks

Log in each day for bonus coins and streak rewards. Daily reminders keep the habit going without being intrusive.

🪙 Coins & Strategy

Earn coins through gameplay or rewarded ads. Spend them on extra moves when you're just one word away from clearing the board.

📶 Fully Offline

All progress is saved locally on your device. No Wi-Fi needed — play on a plane, commute, or anywhere without interruption.

Strategy Meets Language

Word Solitaire MindStack blends the methodical thinking of card solitaire with the creativity of word puzzles. Each hand challenges you to think ahead — which letters to play now, which to hold back — making every board you clear feel genuinely earned.
