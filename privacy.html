Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Word Solitaire MindStack respects your privacy. We collect minimal data to provide the best possible gaming experience.

  • Game Progress: Your level progress, coins, and streaks are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • Settings Preferences: Sound and haptic preferences are saved locally.
  • No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information.

2. Analytics & Advertising

  • We may use third-party analytics services to understand general app usage patterns.
  • Advertisements may be displayed using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data according to Google's privacy policy.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings under Privacy → Advertising.

3. Data Storage

  • All game data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not upload your game progress to external servers.
  • Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data.

4. Children's Privacy

Word Solitaire MindStack does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The game is suitable for all ages and contains no objectionable content.

5. Third-Party Services

The application may integrate with the following services:

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchases and app reviews
  • Apple Notifications — daily reward reminders

Each third-party provider has its own privacy policy governing the data it collects.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:
